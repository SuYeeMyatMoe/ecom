Our Story

From a small kitchen to serving thousands of happy customers daily

How It All Began

Founded in 2015, FoodExpress started as a small family restaurant with a big dream: to deliver delicious, home-cooked meals to busy people.

Our founder, Maria Rodriguez, noticed how hard it was for working professionals and families to find time to cook nutritious meals. What began as a simple delivery service from her own kitchen has now grown into a platform connecting hundreds of local restaurants with food lovers across the city.

Today, we're proud to serve over 50,000 customers monthly while maintaining the same commitment to quality, taste, and service that Maria started with.
